About us

We cook the best
tasty food

Experience the heart and soul of Thailand at Saithong Para Thai. Our chefs artfully blend tradition with innovation, bringing you a menu that explores the rich tapestry of Thai cuisine. From street food delights to coastal treasures, each dish captures the essence of Thailand's culinary heritage. Join us in a warm and inviting atmosphere, where genuine Thai hospitality meets exceptional flavors. Saithong Para Thai is not just a restaurant; it's a celebration of Thai culture through the art of exceptional cuisine. Come, savor, and immerse yourself in the true taste of Thailand.
